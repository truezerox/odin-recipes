Pasta Recipe

Description:

This is a simple pasta recipe that you can try at home.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat the olive oil and saut√© the minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
