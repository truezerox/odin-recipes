Lasagna Recipe

Welcome to the Lasagna recipe page!

Here you will find a delicious recipe for making classic lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and garlic until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Let simmer for about 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
