Pizza Recipe

Welcome to the Pizza recipe page! Here you will find a delicious recipe for making homemade pizza.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your choice of toppings evenly over the cheese.
  7. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  10. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before slicing.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade pizza!
